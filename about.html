About ToGo

Learn more about our scheduling app

Our Mission

ToGo is designed to help students efficiently manage their time by organizing tasks, deadlines, and class schedules. With features like task prioritization, deadline notifications, and schedule tracking, our goal is to improve student productivity and reduce stress.

Key Features

  • Class Details Organizer

    Store and access class information such as room numbers, teacher names, and subject details.

  • Task Categorization

    Label tasks as "Optional," "Urgent," or "Important" for better prioritization.

  • Deadline & Class Notifications

    Receive alerts for upcoming deadlines and class reminders.

  • Priority Suggestions

    Get smart suggestions for task priorities based on deadlines and importance.

Cloud Sync

Your data is securely stored in the cloud, allowing you to access your schedule and tasks from any device. Simply log in with your account and all your information will be available.

Privacy & Security

We take your privacy seriously. Your data is encrypted and stored securely. We never share your personal information with third parties.

Responsive Design

ToGo works seamlessly across all your devices - desktop, tablet, and mobile. The responsive design ensures a great user experience no matter what device you're using.
